In the following, we declare the fixity of two operators.

\begin{code}%
\>[0][@{}l@{\AgdaIndent{1}}]%
\>[2]\AgdaKeyword{infix}%
\>[15I]\AgdaNumber{5}%
\>[16I]\AgdaPostulate{\_+\_}%
\>[17I]\AgdaPostulate{\_*\_}\<%
\end{code}

A fixity declaration can preceed the actual declaration of the names.

\begin{code}%
\>[0][@{}l@{\AgdaIndent{2}}]%
\>[2]\AgdaKeyword{postulate}%
\>[18I]\AgdaPostulate{\_+\_}%
\>[19I]\AgdaPostulate{\_*\_}%
\>[20I]\AgdaSymbol{:}%
\>[21I]\AgdaPrimitiveType{Set}\<%
\end{code}
