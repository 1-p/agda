In the following, we declare the fixity of two operators.

\begin{code}%
\>[0][@{}l@{\AgdaIndent{1}}]\<[2]%
\>[2]\AgdaKeyword{infix}\<[15I]%
\>[15I]\AgdaNumber{5}\<[16I]%
\>[16I]\AgdaPostulate{\_+\_}\<[17I]%
\>[17I]\AgdaPostulate{\_*\_}\<%
\end{code}

A fixity declaration can preceed the actual declaration of the names.

\begin{code}%
\>[0][@{}l@{\AgdaIndent{2}}]\<[2]%
\>[2]\AgdaKeyword{postulate}\<[18I]%
\>[18I]\AgdaPostulate{\_+\_}\<[19I]%
\>[19I]\AgdaPostulate{\_*\_}\<[20I]%
\>[20I]\AgdaSymbol{:}\<[21I]%
\>[21I]\AgdaPrimitiveType{Set}\<%
\end{code}
